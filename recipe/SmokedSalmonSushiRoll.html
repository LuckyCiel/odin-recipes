Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll Recipe

Homemade salmon roll

made with sushi rice, nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi. It might take a while to learn how to make a perfect roll, but you'll have fun trying! Add wasabi (Japanese horseradish) for a good kick!

List of Ingredients

  • rice
  • wasabi paste
  • cucumber
  • avocado

Recipes steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice, then mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
  2. Assemble the sushi rolls based on the detailed recipe below.
  3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces.
